# Account Deletion Policy

## Overview
Your privacy and control over your data are important to us. This policy explains what happens when you choose to delete your account and how to request account deletion.

## What Happens When You Delete Your Account
When you delete your account:

1. **Permanent Data Removal**  
   All data associated with your account, including saved events, reactions, comments, ratings, reviews, and other user-generated content, will be permanently deleted. Personalized settings and history will also be removed.

2. **Vendor Data**  
   For vendor accounts, any payments made will not be refunded. All account information and user-generated content will be permanently deleted and no longer visible.

3. **Access Termination**  
   You will lose access to your account and all app-related services linked to it.

4. **Profile Removal**  
   Your username and profile information will be removed from the app.

5. **Irrecoverability**  
   Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered.

## Contact for Questions or Concerns
If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance, please contact us at **[support email/contact link]**.

## Confirmation
By proceeding with account deletion, you acknowledge that you understand the consequences and confirm your request to delete your account.
