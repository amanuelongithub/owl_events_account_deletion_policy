Account Deletion Policy

Overview

Your privacy and control over your data are important to us. This policy explains what happens when you choose to delete your account and how to request account deletion.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

  • Permanent Data Removal: All data associated with your account, including saved events, reactions, comments, ratings, reviews, and other user-generated content, will be permanently deleted. Personalized settings and history will also be removed.
  • Vendor Data: For vendor accounts, any payments made will not be refunded. All account information and user-generated content will be permanently deleted and no longer visible.
  • Access Termination: You will lose access to your account and all app-related services linked to it.
  • Profile Removal: Your username and profile information will be removed from the app.
  • Irrecoverability: Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered.

Contact for Questions or Concerns

If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance, please contact us at [support email/contact link].

Confirmation

By proceeding with account deletion, you acknowledge that you understand the consequences and confirm your request to delete your account.
